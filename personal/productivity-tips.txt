PRODUCTIVITY TIPS

Time Management:
- Use the Pomodoro Technique (25 min work, 5 min break)
- Block time for deep work sessions
- Limit context switching
- Set specific goals for each day
- Review and plan at the end of each week

Focus:
- Eliminate distractions (turn off notifications)
- Use website blockers during work time
- Create a dedicated workspace
- Use noise-canceling headphones or background music
- Take regular breaks to maintain focus

Task Management:
- Break large tasks into smaller subtasks
- Prioritize using Eisenhower Matrix (urgent/important)
- Use the 2-minute rule (do it now if it takes < 2 min)
- Batch similar tasks together
- Review and update task list regularly

Energy Management:
- Work on difficult tasks when energy is highest
- Take care of physical health (sleep, exercise, nutrition)
- Schedule breaks throughout the day
- Avoid multitasking
- Know when to step away

Learning:
- Learn in focused sessions
- Take notes by hand for better retention
- Teach others to reinforce understanding
- Practice regularly (deliberate practice)
- Review material at spaced intervals

Tools:
- Use a task management system (Todoist, Notion, etc.)
- Calendar blocking for important work
- Time tracking to understand where time goes
- Note-taking app for capturing ideas
- Automation for repetitive tasks
